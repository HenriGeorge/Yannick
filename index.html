VIDEO GAME — AUDIO DIRECTION

Monolith

Three hours of adaptive score that darkens as the player descends. The mix sheds one instrument every level, until only the sub-bass and the player’s footsteps remain.

05 — ABOUT

Sound is the first sense we trust and the last we notice. I’ve spent fifteen years scoring films, games and spaces — chasing the moment an audience stops hearing the music and starts feeling the room.

Based in Belgium — a.k.a. jeanre. Trained as a cellist, ruined by synthesizers. My studio is half chamber ensemble, half machine shop — and every project leaves with a sound no other project will ever get.
